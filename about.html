About Me

Discover my journey, my passion, and my vision for the future.

Who I Am

My name is Sanjay Karmur, and I am a passionate technophile who thrives at the intersection of creativity and technology. From an early age, I developed a fascination for how devices, software, and networks function — and over time, that curiosity evolved into a professional pursuit.

Currently, I am pursuing my Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA), where I continue to expand my knowledge in programming, databases, networking, and operating systems. Alongside academics, I actively build my portfolio through real-world testing, bug reporting, and small development projects.

My Expertise

  • Smartphone Testing: Hands-on experience in identifying usability flaws, system bugs, and performance bottlenecks.
  • Bug Hunting: Reported 275+ valid feedbacks and issues that improved software quality.
  • Web Development: Designing and developing clean, responsive websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Networking & Security: Deep interest in ethical hacking, system vulnerabilities, and preventive measures.

My Journey

Over the past few years, I have not only tested pre-release devices but also gained recognition for reporting accurate, impactful feedback that shaped product development. This journey taught me the importance of precision, attention to detail, and persistence — qualities that define my professional approach.

My Vision

My ultimate goal is to leverage my skills to achieve financial independence and establish myself as a respected professional in the technology sector. I aim to contribute meaningfully to innovation, whether through software testing, development, or creating digital solutions that solve real-world problems.

I believe in continuous learning, adaptability, and resilience — values that guide me every day. With dedication and effort, I am confident in building a net worth of at least 10 Crores in my lifetime while inspiring others through my journey.

Beyond Technology

While technology excites me, I also believe in spirituality, discipline, and balance. Rooted in my Hindu faith yet respectful of all religions, I value integrity, culture, and community. These values influence not just my personal life but also the way I approach professional challenges.
